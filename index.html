Skyblock - Carpentry 50 Calculator

This program calculates the number of Condensed Fermento to craft to reach Carpentry level 50 based on your current carpentry level. It takes the current price of fermento on the bazaar, and any Carpentry Wisdom modifiers like Derpy or skill exp boosting events.

Assume the current price of fermento is 256000 coins(may vary)

Current skill exp modifiers active:

Provides +50 Carpentry wisdom when elected mayor

Gain 15% more Carpentry wisdom while inside the Magma Fields (only legendary variant, level 100)

Gain +25 Carpentry wisdom if a booster cookie is consumed
